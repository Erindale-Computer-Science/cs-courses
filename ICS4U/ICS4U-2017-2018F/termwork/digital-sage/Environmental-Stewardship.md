## Task(s)
According to environmental resources, we are approaching the _Sixth Mass Extinction Event_ ([source][1], [source][2], [source][3], [source][4], [source][5], [source][6],) within the next 20-150 years (depending on source).

After reading through the attached articles:
1. Contact multiple local and governmental agencies to gather information on what they are doing to help the environment (in relation to electronics).
2. Create a list of strategies on how to reduce the impact of computers (and related technologies) on the environment.
3. Create a list of strategies on how to reduce the impact of computers (and related technologies) on human health.

Once you have finished the above, create a report in **any** format of your choosing.  Be prepared to discuss the report with your teacher in a one-on-one scenario.

You will need to include proof of contact with external organizations.
